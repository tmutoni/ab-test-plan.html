AB-004

Execution Framework

  • Framework & tooling: Optimizely (or internal ClickUp variant service) wired to billing front-end.
  • Segmentation: 50 / 50 randomised; power-users tagged separately for secondary cut.
  • Stat confidence: 95 % two-tailed; minimum detectable effect ≥ 4 pp.
  • Sample size: ≈ 4 k completed sessions per variant (≈ 5-7 days at current swag-checkout traffic).
  • Run duration: 14-day cap or earlier on significance.

AB-004 · Error-Message Tone — Demo Flow

Step 1 · Field blur error

Variant A: “Please enter a valid 16-digit card number.”
Variant B: “Hmm, that card # looks short—try all 16 digits 😊”

Step 2 · First failed submit

A: Toast — “Please correct the errors to proceed with payment.”
B: Toast — “We can’t finish the payment just yet—let’s fix the fields in red and you’ll be good to go!”

Step 3 · Second failed submit

B escalates copy → “Need a hand? Double-check card date & CVC.”

Step 4 · Third failed submit

B shows CTA “Chat with support” (opens mock support modal).

Step 5 · Successful retry

B adds 1-sec celebration micro-copy — “Nice! Details look perfect—next stop, review 🚀”
