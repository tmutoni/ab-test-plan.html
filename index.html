Execution Framework

  • Framework & tooling: Optimizely (or internal ClickUp variant service) wired to billing front-end.
  • Segmentation: 50 / 50 randomised; power-users tagged separately for secondary cut.
  • Stat confidence: 95 % two-tailed; minimum detectable effect ≥ 4 pp.
  • Sample size: ≈ 4 k completed sessions per variant (≈ 5-7 days at current swag-checkout traffic).
  • Run duration: 14-day cap or earlier on significance.
